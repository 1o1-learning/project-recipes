Directions

  1. Step 1

    In a small bowl, whisk soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.

  2. Step 2

    In a large skillet over high heat, heat oil. Cook broccoli, bell pepper, and garlic, stirring frequently, until softened, about 5 minutes.
    Add chicken and cook, tossing occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through, about 8 minutes. Stir in cashews; season with pepper.

  3. Step 3

    Pour sauce into skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. honey
  • 2 tsp. toasted sesame oil
